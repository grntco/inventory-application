/ - home (view categories or items, probably in nav bar)


CATEGORY ROUTE
/categories - shows all categories

then either
/category/:id
/categories/?categoryId=id
to show all items in that category


ITEMS ROUTE
one items view for all, by category, by search
/items - all items

then either
/item/:id
/items/?itemId=id
to show an individual item




EXTRA:
/items?search=asdf SEARCH in nav bar
